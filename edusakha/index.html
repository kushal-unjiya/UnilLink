About Us

UniLink

The UniLink Events Feature enables universities to host virtual events, such as webinars, Q&A sessions and campus tours directly through the UniLink platform.
The main idea behind these features is to make it easy and interesting for both universities and students to connect during virtual events. The project wants to use technology to overcome distance, improve talking, and give important information to students.
